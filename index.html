About Me

Hi! My name is Robin Kurosawa, and I am currently a senior at Boston University studying (mostly) Computer Science. I've studied a range of programming courses at BU and abroad at the University of Copenhagen and built a bunch of stuff ranging from a simple web server to a multi-layered neural network.
